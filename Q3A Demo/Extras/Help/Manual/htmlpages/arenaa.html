There are three types of armor available in the Arena Eternal. At the start of a match, you won't have any, so you'll have to look for it. Although the normal maximum armor rating you can have is 100, some improvements allow you to increase it beyond this limit, up to a limit of 200. Whenever your armor rating is above 100, it will gradually count down until it reaches that mark, even if you aren't taking damage. Your current armor rating is displayed on the bottom right corner of the screen.
Armor Shard: These fragments improve your armor rating by 5 points, increasing it even beyond the normal maximum of 100 points.
Combat Armor: Donning a yellow combat armor vest improves your armor rating by 50 points.
Heavy Armor: A suit of red heavy armor will increase your armor rating by 100 points.
